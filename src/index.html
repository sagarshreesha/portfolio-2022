About me

Hi! I am Shreesha, a 22 year old Full Stack Engineer from Bangalore. Actively working on projects either at work or on my free time. My mission is to build human focussed apps using the best of what modern tech has to offer.

My view is that tech should work for us. As a developer my main focus is doing just that. I always put that extra effort into making what I'm developing more human. In the process, I always keep an eye out for the latest tech that can help me achieve my goal without making sacrifices. I always try extending my help when it comes to the best solution for a problem, be it my manager in a design meeting, or my mom with her phone.
